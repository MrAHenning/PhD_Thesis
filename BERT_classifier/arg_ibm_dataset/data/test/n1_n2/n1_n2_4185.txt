Israeli citizens in the south have been suffering from rockets being fired at them According to a 2009 Amnesty International report, Scores [of rockets] have struck homes, businesses, schools, other public buildings and vehicles in and around towns and villages in southern Israel. It is purely by chance that in most cases such strikes have not caused death or injury, and the lethal potential of such projectiles should not be underestimated.
