On 27 December 2008,[REF] Israeli F-16 strike fighters launched a series of air strikes against targets in Gaza as a response to a continuous period of rockets fired from the strip to Israel. Various sites being used as weapons depots were struck: police stations, schools, hospitals, UN warehouses, mosques, various Hamas government buildings and other buildings [REF]. Israel said that the attack was a response to Hamas rocket attacks on southern Israel, which totaled over 3,000 in 2008, and which intensified during the few weeks preceding the operation The situation in Gaza is the direct result of Hamas' rule and priorities
