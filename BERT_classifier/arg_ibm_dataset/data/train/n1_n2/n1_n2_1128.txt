The harm consists in the fact that ‘when I die, I am deprived of all of the value of my future’:[REF] I am deprived of all the valuable ‘experiences, activities, projects, and enjoyments’ that I would otherwise have had [REF]. Thus, if a being has a highly valuable future ahead of it—a ‘future like ours’—then killing that being would be seriously harmful and hence seriously wrong [REF].
