The Organisation Internationale pour le Droit à l'Education et la Liberté d'Enseignement (OIDEL), a international non-profit organization for the development of freedom of education, maintains that the right to education is a fundamental human right which cannot exist without the presence of State benefits and the protection of individual liberties.
