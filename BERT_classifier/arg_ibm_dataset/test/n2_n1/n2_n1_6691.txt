On June 26, 2008, in District of Columbia v. Heller,[REF] the United States Supreme Court affirmed, by a 5-4 vote, the decision of the D.C. Circuit Court of Appeals [REF]. This decision struck down the D.C. gun law. It also clarifies the scope of the Second Amendment to the United States Constitution, stating that it stipulates an individual right irrespective of membership in a militia Americans have an individual right described in the Second Amendment to possess firearms
