Asylum seekers are motivated by the availability of benefits The gaps in the identification process presented opportunities to fraudsters to make multiple applications in various identities so as to duplicate payment of benefits; in 1991, for instance, eight asylum applicants were found to have made 100 asylum and social security applications between them.
