Circumcision provides important health advantages which outweigh the risks Gunsar et al. reported on a prospective study of 50 boys. The periurethral and glanular sulcus flora were evaluated before and after circumcision. Pathogenic bacteria were identified in periurethral swabs taken from 64% of patients before circumcision and 10% after.
