Human beings are equal; cultures are not In 1999, the legal philosopher Paul Cliteur attacked multiculturalism in his book The Philosophy of Human Rights [REF]. Cliteur rejects all political correctness on the issue: Western culture, the Rechtsstaat (rule of law), and human rights are superior to non-Western culture and values. They are the product of the Enlightenment. Cliteur sees non-Western cultures not as merely different but as anachronistic
