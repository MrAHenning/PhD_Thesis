The April 1 election was perhaps the most promising moment of reform. Led by Aung San Suu Kyi, the National League for Democracy won 44 of 45 seats up for grab. Although only a small fraction of the seats were up for a vote, the previously illegal NLD was allowed to campaign, run, and win for the first time.
