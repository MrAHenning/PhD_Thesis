On 15 February 2006, the European Parliament accepted a resolution which condemns all violence arising from the publication of the cartoons. It stated that the EU stands in solidarity with Denmark and all other countries that have been affected by the violence. Furthermore it stated that Muslims may be offended by the cartoons and that they have the right to protest peacefully.
